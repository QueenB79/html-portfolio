The best movies according to Bonnie

Bonnie's top 3 movies

City of angels

A heartwarming romance that explores love, sacrifice, and the beauty of human emotions through the story of an angel who wishes to experience life as a human.

Erin Brokovich

A powerful, inspiring true story of a determined woman fighting against corporate injustice, showcasing resilience and the pursuit of justice.

The Incredibles

A fun, action-packed animated film about a family of superheroes rediscovering their powers and the importance of working together to save the world.
